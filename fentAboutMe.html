ABOUT ME

I am Samuel Westmoreland, a student at Oakland Technical High School in California. I decided to make this website as my Capstone project because Fentanyl and drug abuse is such a major problem in this country and has affected multiple people in my life. I think it is valuable to inform others about the dangers of such a serious drug.
